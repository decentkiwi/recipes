Spinach and Mushroom Quiche

Ingredients for crust

  • 150g all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 3-4 Tbsp water

For filling

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of half & half
  • 100g of shredded cheese
  • 200g spinach
  • 200g mushroom of choice (shiitake / oyster recommended)
  • 1/4 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 Tbsp thyme leaves

Crust making

  1. Add flour and salt to bowl and mix.
  2. Add butter and mix.
  3. Slowly add in water by tablespoon until a dough is formed.
  4. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap. Press the dough into a disc and refridgerate.
  5. Dust your work surface with flour. Roll it out to about 5mm thickness and 5cm wider than your pan.
  6. Place the dough into the pan and ensure that the dough is against the sides of the pan.
  7. Refridgerate the dough for 30 min.
  8. Line the pan with parchment paper and fill it with pie weights (sugar or salt works too).
  9. Bake the tart at 190°C for 25 minutes. Remove the parchment paper and weights and continue to bake for another 5 to 10 minutes until the pastrty is pale golden.
  10. Remove the excess crust.
  11. Let the pastry completely cool.

Filling/Custard making

  1. Cut the mushrooms into bite size pieces.
  2. Coarsely chop the spinach.
  3. Thinly slice the onions and mince the garlic.
  4. In a large pan, add some olive oil. Add mushrooms and cook until browned.
  5. To the same pan, add the onion and garlic, stirring often, until softened. Add spinach, stirring constantly, until wilted and remove from heat.
  6. To a mixing bowl, add eggs, milk, half & half. Whisk until homogenous.
  7. Mix in the mushroom and spinach.
  8. Add about half of the shredded cheese on to the base of the quiche shell.
  9. Pour the custard mixture onto the quiche shell.
  10. Bake at 190°C for 10 minutes and lower to 170°C and bake for another 15-20 minutes.
  11. Let the quiche rest at room temperature before serving.
